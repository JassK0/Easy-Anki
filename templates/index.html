How to use Easy Anki

  1. Pick a pool or upload your own CSV/JSON of questions and click Start.
  2. Answer the questions — the app tracks streaks and awards points and rank based on performance.
  3. Use the Special review on any pool to rehearse the questions you've answered incorrectly.

Tip: you can use an AI assistant (for example ChatGPT) to convert course notes into an Anki-style CSV of question rows. Example prompt you can use:

Download CSV template 
Convert the following course notes into an Anki-style CSV with columns: Question,OptionA,OptionB,OptionC,OptionD,Answer,Explanation. Make about 20 multiple-choice questions that cover the important concepts. Use clear, short questions and plausible distractors.

[PASTE YOUR NOTES HERE]

After you get the CSV from the assistant, upload it on the home page (you must be signed in to upload). The site will create a pool from the file and you can start practicing immediately.
